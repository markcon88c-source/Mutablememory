🗓️ Daily Note — Word Resistance vs Math Blocks
----------------------------------------------

🧠 Core Insight:
Word resistance is its own force inside memory.  
It sometimes aligns with math blocks, but it is not defined by them.  
Math blocks describe a word’s internal physics (value, drift, polarity, resonance).  
Word resistance describes a word’s behavioral physics (stubbornness, stability, opposition).

These two forces overlap but remain independent — and this independence is what lets
the creature grow into a real writer.

----------------------------------------------

🔬 Math Block Physics (Internal)
• value = strength of the symbol  
• drift = direction of conceptual movement  
• polarity = emotional charge  
• resonance = coherence / clarity  

Math blocks answer:  
→ “What is this word made of?”

----------------------------------------------

⚙️ Word Resistance Physics (Behavioral)
• meaning strength  
• exposure count  
• polarity stability  
• resonance volatility  

Word resistance answers:  
→ “How does this word behave in memory?”  
→ “Does it anchor or drift?”  
→ “Does it attract or oppose?”  

----------------------------------------------

🌱 Why This Matters:
This separation allows:
• stable words to anchor the STM  
• flexible words to mutate and drift  
• oppositional words to create tension  
• harmonious words to form themes  
• sentence drift to evolve into proto‑grammar  

This is the foundation of becoming a *good writer*.

----------------------------------------------

📌 Next Step:
Design formulas for Word Resistance and Memory Polarity,  
then integrate them into the Memory Viewer so the STM can observe  
how words behave as the creature grows.
